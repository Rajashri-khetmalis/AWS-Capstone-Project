About the Project

BloodBridge is a cloud-enabled Blood Bank Management System designed to minimize delays during emergency blood requirements. It digitally connects donors, hospitals, and administrators through a single secure platform.

This project is developed as part of an AWS Capstone Project, focusing on scalability, reliability, and real-world healthcare impact.
